The Battleground Brewers Guild (BBG) is Greensboro, North Carolina's premier homebrew club. Founded in November of 2000 we chose to use Battleground in our name because Battleground Avenue is a major road in Greensboro that passes the site of an important Revolutionary war battle between the American general, Nathaniel Greene and British General, Cornwallis. We enjoy gathering at a brewery, pub, or home, to discourse about anything, but mostly sampling and discussing beer. We exchange ideas on brewing and brewing equipment in hopes of advancing our skills as brewers.

The monthly meetings cover topics such as: making and judging beer styles, ingredient use, brewing methods, yeast preparation, and troubleshooting beer flavors. Members are encouraged to bring beer to share with the other members and get feedback (if desired). Our club calendar will reflect other meetings and events.
